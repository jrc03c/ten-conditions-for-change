ACTION SEQUENCE

A sequence of actions sufficient for succeeding in exercising once a week.

Make a list of exercises you'd enjoy.
Choose one of these types of exercise to do weekly.
Pick a time to do the exercise that won't interfere with other obligations.
Decide where you'll be doing the exercise.
Engage in the exercise at that time and place once a week.

ACTION SEQUENCE

A sequence of actions sufficient for succeeding in donating to charity annually.

Assess how much money to donate.
Find out which charities have been proven to be cost-effective ways of helping the world.
Choose a charity to donate to.
Select a date on which to donate.
Set up a recurring donation to occur each year on that date.
